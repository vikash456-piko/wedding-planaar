About Vivah Kro

Vivah Kro was founded by Mr. Vikash Kumar, a passionate entrepreneur and a Bachelor student at the prestigious KIET Group of Institutions. With a vision to simplify and enhance the wedding planning experience, he turned his dream into a platform that helps couples plan their perfect day effortlessly.

At Vivah Kro, we understand that your wedding day is one of the most important moments of your life. Our mission is to take the stress out of wedding planning so you can focus on creating memories that last a lifetime.

We provide a comprehensive range of services, from venue selection and decoration to catering and photography. With a team of dedicated professionals and trusted vendors, we ensure every detail is handled with care and precision.

Our Core Values

  • Customer First: Your happiness is our top priority.
  • Excellence: We strive for perfection in every event we plan.
  • Innovation: Bringing creative ideas to life to make your wedding unique.
